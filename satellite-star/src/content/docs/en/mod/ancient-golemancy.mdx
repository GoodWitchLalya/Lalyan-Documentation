## <span style="color:#e03e2d">Issues</span>

If you're having issues with the mod please, contact me on goodwitchlalya@gmail.com or on [curseforge](https://www.curseforge.com/members/goodwitchlalya/).

When you report a bug be sure to write:

- Which of my mods you are referring to.
- The version of the mod you're using.
- An image or a video of the problem (if you can).
- The log (it would be very userful).
- The other mods you're using.
